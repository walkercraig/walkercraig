title: Sailor's Song

<br/>

[Open Tuned Acoustic Guitar]

[Verse 1]
Sunshine, follow me  
So I can clearly see  
That horizon trouble free  
And my mind can be at ease

[Verse 2]
Starlight, come in close  
It’s you I need the most  
Be my guide to the coast  
Back to my baby and my ghosts

[Verse 3]
Shoreline, be a friend  
Never break and never bend  
Through the tides that never end  
And what they take and what they send

[Verse 4]
Firelight, burn high  
Burn bright through the sea and sky
Stand your ground and testify
Here is home, where my heart lies

[Outro]
If I should lose my way  
And the light should drift away  
Let the winds and waves carry me
To the land of memory and dreams
